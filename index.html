LALCC Flag Bingo Caller

Instructions: Choose the countries to be included for your flag bingo game, the countries that are automatically selected are used for Central Connecticut State University's LALCC flag bingo games, and a copy of their cards can be obtained by using the contact info at the bottom of the page. Once the countries are selected, click "Start" to begin the bingo game, and use the call button to call a new flag until someone says "Bingo". Game will restart when all flags have been called.
You can reset the game sooner by simply refreshing the page (F5)
