What is Flag Bingo?

Flag Bingo is just like bingo with a twist, the squares are the different countries of the world! We provide a bingo caller that randomly calls countries. However, It is not a virtual bingo game. You will be able to print as many bingo cards as you like through the site.

Starting Off

Choose the countries you'd like by clicking the checkboxes next to them. When you are finished, click "Save Game", in the bottom right of your screen. You will then be prompted to name your card set.

Click "Saved Cards" in the top navigation bar.

Select between playing, printing bingo cards, editing your country set, or deleting the set.
