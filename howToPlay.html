What is Flag Bingo?

Flag Bingo is bingo with a twist. Instead of letters and numbers, the squares hold the flags and names of the world's nations. However, It is not a virtual bingo game. You may choose which countries to include and then print your own bingo cards. You will be able to print as many bingo cards as you like through the site. The system then provides you with a bingo caller based on your selections. The caller will randomly call countries. Good luck!

To start:

Click on "Generate Game". Choose the countries that you would like to include in your bingo game. Save the game with an easily identifiable name. Print your bingo cards. When you are ready to play, click on "Play". Click "Call" to get a random country. Then, keep on going until someone gets bingo.

Click "Saved Cards" in the top navigation bar.

Select between playing, printing bingo cards, editing your country set, or deleting the set.
